Scouting App 6.0.0

Date: February 21, 2024

Major Changes
- Adapted the app to utilize features meant for FRC's 2024 game: Crescendo
- Usage of an internal calculator that can crunch through all of the scouted data
- Local storage of all of the scouting data on a user's device for future broadcasting
- Optimized the user interface with additional customization options
- Adaptive layout for the scouting form
- A new data format (DUC) alongside CSV QR Codes that allows seamless data transfers between multipler users
